About Nursery Mitra

Hello! I am Tanmay Nagawade, the developer behind this website and the mobile app named Nursery Mitra. I started developing this project in 2024 with the goal of providing a comprehensive platform to help nurseries manage their business effectively.

This application aims to simplify tasks such as order management, customer relations, and product tracking to empower nursery owners and customers alike. I am committed to continuously improving this platform to meet the evolving needs of its users.
